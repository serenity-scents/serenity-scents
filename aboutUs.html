Mission

"Our group chose to start a scented candle business because we wanted to combine creativity with practicality, offering something meaningful and comforting to others. Candles symbolize warmth, relaxation, and personal expression, and we saw an opportunity to create unique, handcrafted products that resonate with these values.

We were also inspired by the growing demand for eco-friendly and personalized items, which aligns with our passion for sustainability and individuality. By starting this business, we can bring joy and a sense of calm into people's lives while using our skills to craft products that reflect both quality and care."
